Slide 03 // The Solution

ZK compliance
that never sees your data.

🔐
Zero-Knowledge Proofs

Groth16-shaped proofs prove set-membership. Your PII is hashed into a commitment — only the hash touches the chain.

🌲
Merkle Tree Verification

Commitments form a Merkle tree. The root is stored on-chain. Proof = "I'm in the tree" without revealing which leaf.

⚡
Autonomous Revocation

CSPR.cloud streaming monitor triggers revoke on the Odra contract the instant a risk event fires.

🖥️
Compliance Console

Slide 04 // Core Flow

Compliance Lifecycle

01 · Issue Credential

Issuer hashes PII into commitment off-chain

02 · Merkle Commitment

Commitment inserted into on-chain Merkle tree via Odra contract

03 · Generate ZK Proof

Holder generates Groth16-shaped proof of set-membership

04 · Verify Proof

Verifier checks proof + nullifier — no PII revealed

05 · Monitor (Autonomous)

CSPR.cloud streaming watches for risk events 24/7

06 · Auto-Revoke

Risk detected → nullifier registered → proof instantly invalidated
