# Suno/Udio Music Vibe Prompt: Bastion

### Background Vibe
- **Genre:** Cyberpunk Synthwave / Dark Ambient Electro
- **Style:** Mid-tempo arpeggiated bass lines, cold digital synthesizer pads, TR-808 snare hits, clinical sweeps, high-contrast security operations center (SOC) terminal aesthetic.
- **Vocals:** None (Strictly Instrumental, No Singing, No Voice Background)
- **Instruments:** Analog synthesizers, industrial drum machines, pulsing sub-bass, cybernetic glitch sweeps.
- **Reference Tracks:** Deus Ex Soundtrack (Michael McCann), Daft Punk (Tron Legacy), Gesaffelstein.
- **Mood Shift:** Begins with steady, tense electronic ticks (compliance audit threat), building into a cold arpeggiating pulse (console and proof trace inspection), dropping into a sharp high-bass digital distortion (sanctions alert and screen shake), rising to a driving cybernetic rhythm (testnet transaction broadcast), and resolving to a clean ambient drone.
